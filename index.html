Why you should’t cracked software? As a student, I can say that I used to think piracy was the solution until I learned about open source and free alternatives (see https://alternativeto.net/) and that pirated applications could contain viruses. Generally, hacker groups cracking applications in pirated software tend to avoid adding viruses so as not to tarnish their reputation. However, the probability of viruses being added to pirated applications is quite high until they reach websites accessible to end users in Turkey.
